Our Facilities

World Class Library

The Darlene Columbia University Libraries is a system of twenty-two libraries with extensive print and electronic resources, innovative services, and expert staff teamed with a group of academic technology centers supporting instruction and scholarship.

Rec Plex/Center

Fitness Center with cardiovascular and strength-training equipment. Indoor rock-climbing and bouldering walls. Outdoor free-standing boulder for climbing.

Healthy and Delicious Dining

Resident students that live on campus, will select their meal plan when completing their housing contract. Resident students that live in university-owned apartments are able to purchase their meal plan online or in our main office.
